# Executive findings - Playzone Product Analytics

## Decision summary

Playzone's largest measurable gain is not only bringing more users into the top of the funnel. The main issue is the path between discovering an opportunity, sending an invitation and confirming a booking. The ordered funnel shows that 5.6% of users who open the product reach the value moment. The largest drop happens at **Invitation sent**, where 64.8% of users are lost from the previous step.

## What the data shows

- Users analyzed: 500
- Ordered activation rate: 5.6%
- Confirmed bookings: 28
- Confirmation rate after invitation: 62.2%
- Average time from invitation to confirmation: 41.9 hours
- Best channel by ordered activation: Social (10.0%)
- Weakest channel by ordered activation: Organic (2.5%)
- Strongest category after invitation: Games (72.7%)
- Critical data quality failures: 0

## Product recommendations

1. Prioritize the drop at **Invitation sent** before increasing acquisition investment.
2. Compare acquisition channels using Social as the activation benchmark and investigate the promise/onboarding of Organic.
3. Treat confirmed booking as the activation definition and monitor D1, D7 and D30 retention separately for activated and non-activated users.
4. Use category liquidity to decide where to improve offer, reminders, recommendations or availability.
5. Keep quality checks in place before presenting activation metrics to leadership.
